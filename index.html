About Me

Edgar was born in Monterrey, Mexico. He has a degree as an Industrial Engineer from the University of Nuevo Leon. He worked for many years in different companies mostly in Quality related positions. His working experience covers a wide variety of fields like: Brewery, Flat Glass, Automotive, Electronics and Education.

After moving to the U.S. and working in Education, and recently completed a Web Design Certificate in SMU last year 2016 and being exposed to basic code with front end technologies he has decided to go one step further and apply for the SMU coding bootcamp. He believes that this actions will give him a better posibility to enhance his career and make the final desired transition into the Tech field.
